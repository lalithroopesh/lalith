Involvement and Opportunities for USF students!

  • Bulls Science Olympiad is looking for volunteers to help write science tests for a K-12 STEM competition we are hosting virtually from USF! No prior experience with Science Olympiad is needed, and you only need an interest in STEM to be able to write. We offer volunteer hours and merch for writing, supervising virtually, and grading. To sign up, please fill out this form. We are also hosting a Test-Writing Workshop on November 8th in collaboration with SASE, and it'd be great if you can attend!
  • EBC is hosting a research seminar on November 22nd at the Oval Theater in collaboration with URS to both exhibit USF students' projects and introduce students to ways to get into research! We are looking for presenters, so if you have research to present, feel free to sign-up via this form. If you are just interested in attending, here is the BullsConnect event link with more details and an RSVP.

10/21/2022: Hello world!

This marks the first creation of this iteration of my website! It is actually somewhat put together and I am actively learning (well, re-learning) my HTML, CSS, and JS skills. I think that website-building coding languages are somewhat dead due to the sheer amount of website creation sites and services, but it is a good skill to know if you need to make modifications, which I have many times. Custom sites are especially common in academia, from very drab .edu lecture notes repositories to "meet the team"s upheld by individual research labs. As for me, this is just a fun project and a great place to write down and easily access what I'm doing. I'll also be using it as a testing grounds for anything else I can host, so feel free to check back whenever!
